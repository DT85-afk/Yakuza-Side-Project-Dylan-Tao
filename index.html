Why you should play Yakuza

Yakuza is a fun game with fun combat

The combat in the game, although it appears to be a simple button masher, there is more depth for players that are looking for it. The best thing about that depth is that unlike some games it isn't too hard to perform and execute. It's just a lot of knowing and knowledge with the game's systems.

For example here is a list of the varied fighting styles in one of the games Yakuza 0. Here you can switch between them in order to suit various situations similar to a game such as Ghost of Tsushima

  • Brawler
  • Rush

  • Beast
  • Dragon of Dojima

Fun Characters!

This game is chock full of memorable and fun characters

This game is chock full of characters such as Kiryu, as you go through his journey you begin with how the character grows throughout the story, how he views the world through his lense, as well as learn how this man becomes the Dragon of Dojima

Wacky Substories!

Wonderful Sidequests

This game is full of wonderful sidequests that is sure to either make people laugh, or tug on their heartstrings a little with beautifully written substories, that goes a long way to make each game memorable!
